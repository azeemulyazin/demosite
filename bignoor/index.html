About Us

Bignoor – Your Neighbourhood Supermarket

Located conveniently on Kannur Airport Road, Valiyannur, Bignoor Supermarket has been a trusted name for families across the region. We bring together the widest range of fresh produce, groceries, household essentials, and specialty items under one roof.

Our mission is simple — offer the best quality products at fair prices, with a shopping experience that feels warm, welcoming, and truly local.

  • Freshness Guaranteed — Daily restocked fresh fruits, vegetables, and dairy products.
  • Best Prices — Competitive pricing on all items with regular offers and deals.
  • Prime Location — Easily accessible on Kannur Airport Road, near Varam P.O.
  • Friendly Service — A welcoming team always ready to help you find what you need.
